This problem was particularly interesting because it combined mathematical reasoning with a real-world AI development scenario. Instead of simulating every possible strategy, the key was to compare two independent approaches and choose the faster one. 💡

🔍 Problem Understanding
Maxim develops the project continuously at a speed of x lines/hour.
Nikita has two choices:
1️⃣ Start coding immediately at y lines/hour.
2️⃣ Spend z hours setting up an AI agent 🤖, after which productivity increases to 10 × y lines/hour.
The goal is to determine the minimum time required to complete a project consisting of n lines of code.

🧠 Key Observation
Instead of simulating the coding process hour by hour, we can directly calculate the completion time for both strategies.
Strategy 1: No AI Setup
Both developers work from the beginning.
Combined speed:
x+yx + yx+yTime required:
⌈nx+y⌉\left\lceil \frac{n}{x+y} \right\rceil⌈x+yn​⌉Strategy 2: Use AI
Nikita first spends z hours setting up the AI.
During these z hours, only Maxim contributes:
x×zx \times zx×zlines of code.
Case 1: Project finishes during setup
If
x×z≥nx \times z \ge nx×z≥nthen the project is completed before the AI is even ready.
Time:
⌈nx⌉\left\lceil \frac{n}{x} \right\rceil⌈xn​⌉Case 2: AI becomes active
Remaining work:
n−xzn - xzn−xzAfter setup, the combined speed becomes:
x+10yx + 10yx+10yAdditional time:
⌈n−xzx+10y⌉\left\lceil \frac{n-xz}{x+10y} \right\rceil⌈x+10yn−xz​⌉Total time:
z+⌈n−xzx+10y⌉z + \left\lceil \frac{n-xz}{x+10y} \right\rceilz+⌈x+10yn−xz​⌉

⚡ Final Approach
Compute:
time_without_ai
time_with_ai

and output:
min(time_without_ai, time_with_ai)

Simple, efficient, and mathematically optimal. ✅
📈 Complexity Analysis
Time Complexity: O(1) per test case
Space Complexity: O(1)
No simulation required — just direct calculations.

🎯 Key Learning
This problem reinforces an important competitive programming lesson:
✅ Compare multiple strategies independently.
✅ Use mathematical formulas instead of brute-force simulation.
✅ Always check edge cases where an optimization (AI setup) may not actually be beneficial.
Sometimes the smartest solution is simply evaluating all possible choices and selecting the best one. 🚀
