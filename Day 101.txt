🧠 Problem Idea:
The goal is to determine the minimum number of months required for a plant to grow at least k centimeters, given the growth in each month.

💡 Approach:
If the required growth k = 0, the answer is 0 months.
Otherwise, sort the monthly growth values in descending order.
Accumulate growth month by month until the total growth ≥ k.
Count the number of months used.
If it’s impossible to reach k, output -1.

🔑 Key Learning:
A greedy approach works well here—always pick the largest growth values first to minimize the number of months.
