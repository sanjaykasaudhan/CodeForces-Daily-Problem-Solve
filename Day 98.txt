🧠 Problem Idea:
The string contains comparison symbols (< and >).
The goal is to determine the maximum possible length of a valid sequence based on consecutive identical comparison symbols.

💡 Approach:
Traverse the string and track consecutive runs of the same character.
Maintain the maximum run length encountered.
The final answer becomes max_run + 1, representing the longest valid sequence that can be formed.

🔑 Key Learning:
Sometimes the optimal solution comes from observing patterns in consecutive elements rather than simulating every possibility.
