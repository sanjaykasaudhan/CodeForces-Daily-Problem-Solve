🧩 The Problem: Given an array of n integers, determine if the number 67 appears anywhere in it! Print YES if it does, NO otherwise!
That's literally the entire problem — check for the presence of 67! 😄
The name "Sieve of Erato67henes" is a brilliant pun on the Sieve of Eratosthenes (the ancient prime-finding algorithm), with 67 sneakily embedded in the name! 🏺

Examples:
n=5, a=[1,7,6,7,67] → 67 is in a → YES ✅
n=5, a=[1,3,5,7,8] → 67 not in a → NO ✅
