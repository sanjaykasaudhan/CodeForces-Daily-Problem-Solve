🧠 Problem Insight
Given a number of candies n, we need to find the maximum number of people such that:
Candies can be evenly distributed
Each person gets a number following the form (2ᵏ − 1)

💡 Solution Approach
Iterate over powers of 2
Check divisibility of n by (2ᵏ − 1)
The first valid division gives the required answer
Efficient and elegant use of bit manipulation 💡

🚀 Key Learnings
Powers of 2 appear frequently in competitive problems
Bitwise thinking simplifies math-heavy logic
Div-3 problems are great for strengthening fundamentals
