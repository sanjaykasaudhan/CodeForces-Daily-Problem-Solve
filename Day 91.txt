🧠 Problem Insight
The task was to compute the value of an array, defined as the sum of maximums of all prefixes.
 Additionally, we were allowed to swap any two elements at most once to maximize this value.

Understanding how element positioning affects prefix contributions
Brute-force swap checking works due to small constraints (Div. 4)
